## Hello!

I'm Jerry Kaufman, a student at Champlain College, where I've been studying for a B.S Game Programming degree with a minor in mathematics with a current specialization in Systems and Tools development. I enjoy getting to develop pipelines and systems that are cross-discipline friendly. Whether that's creating resources for other team members to reference regarding version control, systems or general documentation that ensure everything is as comprehensible and flexible so that the project can benefit from varying skill levels. 

In my free time, I like to work on my side projects, like Nameless Heroes, and of course, play video games. With some of my all time favorites being Firewatch for the visuals, and the Horizon Series for their deep and engaging narrative through worldbuilding.
